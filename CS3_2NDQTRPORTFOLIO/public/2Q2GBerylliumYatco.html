Meet the Students of 2029 Beryllium

The 2029 Beryllium Student Directory serves as a digital yearbook that showcases the bright and talented students of our class. Each student has their own unique skills, passions, and stories that make Beryllium a fun and creative family. This directory will be a compilation of the memories we will build together throughout the school year.

Here, you can explore the faces and short autobiographies of every member of our section. From our class leaders to our creatives, each profile reflects the aspects that define Beryllium. Take a moment to look through our gallery to get to know the people who make our class truly one of a kind.
